DeepLearning Vision & Text-Based Product Categorizer

Developed in Python using TensorFlow and PyTorch, this project explores and compares various neural network architectures for Amazon product classification. The goal is to predict and assign product categories by leveraging both image recognition and text tokenization models. We tested multiple neural network designs for each modality, comparing their performance in predicting product categories. The dataset, sourced from publicly available Amazon reviews and metadata, was meticulously parsed, split, and cleaned for training and testing. With 10 predefined product categories, the project culminated in an ensemble approach, combining the strengths of both image and text models to optimize prediction accuracy.

Campus Restroom Rating Website - Bruin Baño

Written in HTML and CSS and connected via Javascript to a Firebase database, Bruin Baños is a web application designed to rank and review restrooms across UCLA’s campus. Students can rate their restroom experiences and submit detailed reviews for others to see. With Bruin Baños, users gain valuable insights, helping them make informed decisions on the best campus restrooms to use.
