DeepLearning Vision & Text-Based Product Categorizer

Developed in Python using TensorFlow and PyTorch, this project explores and compares various neural network architectures for Amazon product classification. The goal is to predict and assign product categories by leveraging both image recognition and text tokenization models. We tested multiple neural network designs for each modality, comparing their performance in predicting product categories. The dataset, sourced from publicly available Amazon descriptions and metadata, was meticulously parsed, split, and cleaned for training and testing. With 10 predefined product categories, the project culminated in an ensemble approach, combining the strengths of both image and text models to optimize prediction accuracy.
